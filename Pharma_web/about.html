Our Story

MediCare Pharmacy has been serving the community since 2005, providing quality healthcare products and personalized service.

Founded by Dr. Himanshu Patel, a licensed pharmacist with over 20 years of experience, our pharmacy has grown from a small neighborhood drugstore to a trusted healthcare destination serving thousands of customers annually.

We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare, combining traditional pharmaceutical services with wellness programs and preventive care education.

Quality Medications

We source only from reputable manufacturers and distributors.

Expert Pharmacists

Our team includes licensed professionals with specialized training.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate healthcare services that improve the quality of life for our patients and community. We strive to be more than just a pharmacy - we aim to be your healthcare partner.

Our Vision

To transform community pharmacy practice by integrating clinical services with medication dispensing, creating a model for patient-centered care that can be replicated nationwide.
